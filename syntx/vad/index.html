Types of Verbal Adjectives

Quenya has several types of verbal adjectives (adjectives formed from verb stems):

  • adjectives in -ya
  • adjectives in -ula
  • adjective in -itë
  • adjective in -ima

For the formation of these adjectives, →@@ (Word formation). For the use of participles (which are also a kind of verbal adjective), →52.

Adjectives in -ima

The adjectives in -ima formed from lengthened-grade stem express passive possibility:

Note

‘Passive possibility’ should here be taken to include a range of nuances, such as 'permission', 'right', 'appropriateness', etc.: avaquétima  not to be said, that must not be said

When form from zero-grade stem, the adjective in -ima were (permanently, by nature) intensive:

  •  luminous
  •  wise
  •  affectionate

Unlike the lengthened-grade adjectives, these can be formed also from intransitive verbs.
